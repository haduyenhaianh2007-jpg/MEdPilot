## Nhiễm trùng lây truyền qua đường tình dục

Các nhiễm trùng lây truyền qua đường tình dục (STIs) quan trọng bao gồm:

- [Giang mai](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/syphilis)
- [Lậu](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/gonorrhoea)
- [Lymphogranuloma venereum](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/lymphogranuloma-venereum)
- [Hạ cam mềm](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/chancroid)
- [Granuloma inguinale](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/granuloma-inguinale) (donovanosis)
- [Trichomoniasis](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/trichomoniasis)
- [Mụn rộp sinh dục](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/genital-herpes)
- [Mụn cóc sinh dục](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/anogenital-warts)
- [Molluscum contagiosum](https://dermnetnz.org/topics/molluscum-contagiosum).
